<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><A NAME="a_001"></A><B>Notice of the Annual General Meeting of Nokia Corporation</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white"><B>Notice is given to the shareholders of
Nokia Corporation (&ldquo;Nokia&rdquo; or the &ldquo;Company&rdquo;) of the Annual General Meeting to be held on Thursday, 9 April 2026
at 14:00 EEST at Finlandia Hall, Mannerheimintie 13e, Helsinki, Finland.</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">The reception of persons who have registered
for the Annual General Meeting (&ldquo;Meeting&rdquo;), the distribution of voting tickets, and coffee service will commence at 12:30
EEST.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">Shareholders can also exercise their voting
rights by voting in advance. Instructions for advance voting are presented in this notice under section C.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white"><FONT STYLE="color: black">Shareholders may
follow the Meeting through a webcast. Following the webcast is not considered participation or exercise of shareholders&rsquo; rights
in the Meeting. Instructions regarding the webcast are available in this notice under section C. and later on the Company&rsquo;s website
at </FONT><U>www.nokia.ly/agm2026</U><FONT STYLE="color: black">.</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white"><B>18. Authorization to the Board of Directors
to resolve to repurchase the Company&rsquo;s own shares</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">The Board of Directors proposes that the
Annual General Meeting authorize the Board of Directors to resolve to repurchase a maximum of 550 million shares, which corresponds to
less than 10% of the Company&rsquo;s total number of shares. The repurchases under the authorization are proposed to be carried out by
using funds in the unrestricted equity, as resolved by the Board of Directors, which means that the repurchases will reduce the distributable
funds of the Company.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">The price paid for the shares under the authorization
shall be based on the market price of the Nokia shares on the securities markets on the date of the repurchase or a price otherwise formed
in a competitive process. Shares may be repurchased to be cancelled, held to be reissued, transferred further or for other purposes resolved
by the Board of Directors. The Company may enter into derivative, share lending or other arrangements customary in capital market practice.
The shares may be repurchased otherwise than in proportion to the shares held by the shareholders (directed repurchase). The Board shall
resolve on all other matters related to the repurchase of Nokia shares.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">It is proposed that the authorization be
effective until 8 October 2027 and terminate the authorization for repurchasing the Company&rsquo;s shares granted by the Annual General
Meeting on 29 April 2025 to the extent that the Board has not previously resolved to repurchase shares based on such authorization.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white"><B>19. Authorization to the Board of Directors
to resolve to issue shares and special rights entitling to shares</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">The Board of Directors proposes that the
Annual General Meeting authorize the Board of Directors to resolve to issue in total a maximum of 550 million shares through issuance
of shares or special rights entitling to shares under Chapter 10, Section 1 of the Finnish Limited Liability Companies Act in one or
more issues during the effective period of the authorization. The Board of Directors may issue either new shares or treasury shares held
by the Company. The proposed maximum amount corresponds to less than 10% of the Company&rsquo;s total number of shares.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">Shares and special rights entitling to shares
may be issued in deviation from the shareholders&rsquo; pre-emptive rights within the limits set by law. The authorization may be used
to develop the Company&rsquo;s capital structure, diversify the shareholder base, finance or carry out acquisitions or other arrangements,
settle the Company&rsquo;s equity-based incentive plans or for other purposes resolved by the Board of Directors. The Board of Directors
shall resolve on all terms and conditions of the issuance of shares and special rights entitling to shares under Chapter 10, Section
1 of the Finnish Limited Liability Companies Act.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">It is proposed that the authorization be
effective until 8 October 2027 and terminate the authorization for issuance of shares and special rights entitling to shares resolved
at the Annual General Meeting on 29 April 2025 to the extent that the Board has not previously resolved to issue shares or special rights
based on such authorization.</P>
