<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>Nokia has <FONT STYLE="background-color: white">published its Nokia
in 2025 Annual Report and</FONT> filed its Annual Report on Form&nbsp;20-F for 2025</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><FONT STYLE="background-color: white">Espoo, Finland &ndash; Nokia
has today published its Nokia in 2025 Annual Report, which includes audited financial statements, the annual review by the Board of Directors,
including the Sustainability Statement, Nokia&rsquo;s Corporate Governance Statement and the Remuneration Report for the governing bodies
for 2025. Additionally, Nokia has filed its Annual Report on Form&nbsp;20-F for 2025 with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission.</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><FONT STYLE="background-color: white">The Nokia in 2025 Annual Report
and the Annual Report on Form&nbsp;20-F will be available in PDF format at <U>www.nokia.com/financials</U>. The corporate governance
statement will be available also at <U>https://www.nokia.com/we-are-nokia/leadership-and-governance/</U> and the Remuneration Report
at <U>https://www.nokia.com/about-us/company/leadership-and-governance/remuneration/</U>.</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><FONT STYLE="background-color: white">The financial statements are
also published in XHTML format in accordance with the European Single Electronic Format (ESEF) reporting requirements. In accordance
with ESEF requirements, the consolidated financial statements are marked with iXBRL tags. The audit firm Deloitte Oy has provided an
independent auditor&rsquo;s report on Nokia&rsquo;s ESEF financial statements based on a reasonable assurance engagement it has performed
in accordance with International Standard on Assurance Engagements (ISAE) 3000. The ESEF financial statements in Finnish are available
in the zip file attached to this release and at <U>https://www.nokia.com/about-us/investors/results-reports/</U>.</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><FONT STYLE="background-color: white">Nokia&rsquo;s Sustainability
Statement is published as part of the annual review by the Board of Directors. The Statement is prepared in accordance with European Union
Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive (CSRD), the European sustainability reporting standards as referred to in Chapter 7 of the
Finnish Accounting Act, and Article&nbsp;8 of the EU Taxonomy Regulation. It has been subject to limited assurance by Deloitte Oy, who
has provided an Assurance report in accordance with International Standard on Assurance Engagements ISAE 3000 (revised).</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>
