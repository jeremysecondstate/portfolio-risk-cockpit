<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>Nokia appoints Emma Falck as President of Mobile Infrastructure
and member of the Group Leadership Team</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Espoo, Finland &ndash; Nokia today announced the appointment of Emma
Falck as President of Mobile Infrastructure and member of the Nokia Group Leadership Team, effective 1 September 2026.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><FONT STYLE="font-size: 10pt">Falck brings extensive experience leading
transformation and improving performance in complex global technology businesses. She joins Nokia from Siemens, where she serves as Executive
Vice President, Products, Smart Infrastructure Buildings, leading a global organization across product management and development, and
supply chain. At Siemens, she held senior strategy roles and led technology organizations building automation, software and connected
devices. </FONT>Earlier in her career, she was a Partner and Managing Director at Boston Consulting Group and held senior leadership roles
at KONE. She holds a PhD in Computational Physics from Aalto University.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&ldquo;I&rsquo;m delighted to welcome Emma to Team Nokia. As AI moves
toward physical AI, networks need to become AI-native by design for both 5G Advanced and 6G.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Our focus in Mobile Infrastructure is clear: To help our customers
succeed by building a software-led infrastructure business that leverages open interfaces, standards, and a rich partner ecosystem to
accelerate innovation.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Emma brings broad transformation experience, operational leadership
and a fresh perspective from global businesses that use technology to enable automation. She is the right leader to take MI into this
new chapter,&rdquo; said <B>Justin Hotard</B>, President and CEO of Nokia.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&ldquo;I&rsquo;m excited to join Nokia at a pivotal moment for our
customers and the industry. As networks evolve to support new AI-driven demands, customers need partners who can deliver with speed and
predictability, and turn technology roadmaps into real-world performance.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Mobile Infrastructure&rsquo;s breadth across core software, radio networks
and technology standards is a solid foundation. I look forward to working with the team to strengthen our execution, embed AI into our
development and delivery processes, and bring the next wave of innovation to our customers,&rdquo; said <B>Falck</B>.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Falck will be based in Espoo, Finland and report to Nokia&rsquo;s President
and Chief Executive Officer, Justin Hotard.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><FONT STYLE="font-size: 10pt">Additional background information on
all current members of the Group Leadership Team can be found at:&nbsp;</FONT>www.nokia.com/en_int/investors/corporate-governance/group-leadership-team.</P>
